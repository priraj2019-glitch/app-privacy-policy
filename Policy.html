Privacy Policy of HairTrans AI

Last updated: January 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how HairTrans AI (“we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, processes, and protects information when you use our mobile application and related services (the “Service”).

By using the Service, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information Collection and Use

Information You Provide

  • Photos you voluntarily upload for image processing
  • Messages or inquiries sent through support channels

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information (device type, operating system version)
  • Usage data (features used, session duration)
  • Crash logs and diagnostic data for app stability

2. Face Data Policy

Collection and Purpose

HairTrans AI processes facial data only when you upload an image to apply a requested visual effect such as hairstyle simulation or similar face-based features.

Face data is used solely to:

  • Detect facial feature points (eyes, nose, facial contours)
  • Apply the user-requested visual transformation

The app does not perform facial recognition, biometric identification, or user profiling.

Processing

Uploaded images are processed temporarily and may be transmitted to secure servers only for the purpose of generating the requested result.

Retention (Explicit Statement)

HairTrans AI does NOT retain face data.

  • Facial images and facial feature data are deleted immediately after processing
  • No face data is stored, cached, archived, or reused
  • No face data is retained after the result is delivered to the user

Sharing

Face data is not shared, sold, rented, or disclosed to any third parties and is never used for advertising or analytics purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

The app may use trusted third-party services for cloud infrastructure, analytics, and crash reporting. These providers are contractually required to protect user data and do not independently store or retain face data.

4. Data Security

5. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed outside your country of residence. When applicable, we apply appropriate safeguards to ensure lawful data protection.

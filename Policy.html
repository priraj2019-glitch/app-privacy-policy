Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Face Data

The application may use camera-based functionality for real-time visual interaction. The application does NOT collect, store, retain, or process face data.

  • No facial images or videos are stored.
  • No facial recognition or biometric identification is performed.
  • No face templates, face maps, or facial profiles are created.
  • All camera input is processed only in real time on the device.
  • No face data is retained, uploaded, or transmitted.

Data Collection

The application does not collect personally identifiable information unless explicitly required for core functionality.

Data Storage and Sharing

The application does not store face data locally or on servers and does not share face data with any third parties.

User Privacy

User privacy is a priority. All processing occurs locally on the user's device.
